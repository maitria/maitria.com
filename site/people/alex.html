This is why they pay me the big bucks

Here's some of the special stuff I bring to a team:

  • I am caring, and know how to listen.
  • I cut right to a clear statement of a problem or goal.
  • I ask interesting (hard) questions — often the questions nobody else will bring up.
  • I challenge a pair to write the best code they can.
  • I break things into small steps for fast feedback.
  • I am kind and honest; I do not mislead or sugar-coat.
  • I'm a constant learner, and I encourage teams to learn.
  • I don't much play up my strengths or try to hide my weaknesses. What you see is what you get.
  • When facilitating retrospectives, I encourage real collaboration.
